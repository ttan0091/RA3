# Pocket Quest Examples

## Example 1

Inputs:
- Time: 15 minutes
- Location: office
- Mood: curious
- Theme: coffee

Output:
Quest Title: The Quiet Steam
Objective: Discover a new detail in your workspace you never noticed.
Obstacles:
- Habit fog tries to keep you on autopilot.
- A small distraction pings at the wrong moment.
Ally NPC: Jun the Mug Keeper (always knows where the clean cup hides)
Reward: One fresh note on how to improve your desk flow.
Timebox: 15 minutes
Twist: The detail you find becomes tomorrow's tiny experiment.

## Example 2

Inputs:
- Time: 8 minutes
- Location: train
- Mood: restless

Output:
Quest Title: The Window Runner
Objective: Collect three small patterns outside the window and name them.
Obstacles:
- Station stops reset your attention.
- Your phone begs for a quick scroll.
Ally NPC: Mira the Ticket Archivist (remembers every line)
Reward: A calmer pulse and a quick sketch in your notes.
Timebox: 8 minutes
Twist: One pattern becomes a title for your next playlist.
